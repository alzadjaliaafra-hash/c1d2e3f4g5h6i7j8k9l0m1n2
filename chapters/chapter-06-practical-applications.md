### Murshidi's Recommendation

**When to Rebrand:**
- Current name is non-fluent or confusing
- Current name limits market expansion
- Current name creates negative associations
- Market conditions favor differentiation

**When NOT to Rebrand:**
- Current name has strong brand equity
- Current name is highly fluent
- Rebranding costs exceed expected benefits
- Market conditions favor conformity

**Rebranding Strategy:**

1. **Assess current name fluency**: Can stakeholders pronounce it easily? Do they understand what you do?

**Evidence:** Fenneman et al. (2023) found that fluency increases return expectations by 40-50%. Kellogg School found that deliberate names increase growth by 13%. Barcelona School found that nonconforming names increase ROA by +1%.

### Implementation Steps

1. Conduct stakeholder interviews: What's working and what's not with current name?

3. Define success metrics: What will indicate successful rebranding?
